Hi!

Games in real space is a website dedicated to casual outdoor games, currently focusing on Prospect Park, Brooklyn. The goal is to create a stream of diverse outdoor activities that people can join on a whim—capture the flag, pick-up socker, frisbee, etc—and at the same time to provide a space for people to create and share games of their own.

We live in a world that is more isolated and virtual than ever before. Let's get people together, in person, having fun and moving their bodies— playing games in real space.

Games in real space is invite-only. If you would like to join and do not have an invitation, you can send an email to webmaster@inreal.space.
